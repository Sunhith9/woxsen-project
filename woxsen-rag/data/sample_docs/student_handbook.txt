# Woxsen University – Student Handbook (Excerpt)
## Academic Policies

### Attendance Policy
Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in all courses. Students falling below 75% attendance will be debarred from appearing in the end-semester examinations. Attendance is recorded electronically via the student portal at portal.woxsen.edu.in. Any medical or emergency leave must be communicated to the Academic Office within 3 days with supporting documents.

### Grade System
Woxsen University follows a 10-point GPA grading system:
- O (Outstanding): 90–100 marks → 10 grade points
- A+ (Excellent): 80–89 marks → 9 grade points
- A (Very Good): 70–79 marks → 8 grade points
- B+ (Good): 60–69 marks → 7 grade points
- B (Average): 50–59 marks → 6 grade points
- P (Pass): 40–49 marks → 5 grade points
- F (Fail): Below 40 marks → 0 grade points

### Examination Rules
- Hall tickets must be downloaded from the student portal 3 days before exams begin.
- Students must carry their hall ticket AND ID card to every examination.
- Mobile phones and electronic devices are strictly prohibited in examination halls.
- Late entry beyond 15 minutes from start time is not permitted.
- Malpractice results in immediate disqualification and disciplinary action.

### Revaluation Process
Students can apply for revaluation within 7 days of result publication. The process:
1. Download revaluation form from portal.woxsen.edu.in → Examinations
2. Pay ₹500 per subject at the Accounts Department (Room 201, Admin Block)
3. Submit form + receipt at the Exam Cell, Room 105, Admin Block
4. Results communicated within 21 working days

## Library Rules and Services

### Library Timings
- Monday to Saturday: 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM
- Sunday: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM
- Closed on national holidays and university-declared holidays

### Borrowing Policy
- UG Students: 3 books for 14 days
- PG Students: 5 books for 21 days
- PhD Scholars: 7 books for 30 days
- Faculty: 10 books for 60 days

### Late Return Fines
- ₹2 per book per day for overdue books
- A fine exceeding ₹200 will result in temporary suspension of library privileges
- Fines can be paid at Library Counter 1

### Digital Resources
Students can access JSTOR, Scopus, IEEE Xplore, and Springer through the campus network or via VPN using university credentials. Contact library@woxsen.edu.in for VPN access.

### Lost Library Book
If a borrowed book is lost, the student must replace it with a new copy or pay the current market price of the book + 25% damage charges. Report to Library Counter 3.

## Fee Structure & Payment

### Payment Methods
Fees can be paid via:
1. Online: Student portal → Fee Payment (Net banking, UPI, Credit/Debit card)
2. Demand Draft: In favour of "Woxsen University" payable at Hyderabad
3. In-person: Accounts Department, Room 201, Admin Block

### Fee Deadlines
- Semester 1: Within 30 days of joining
- Subsequent semesters: Before the 10th of the month the semester begins
- Late fee: ₹100 per week after deadline

### Scholarship Disbursement
Scholarships are credited directly to the student's bank account registered on the portal. For discrepancies, contact the Scholarship Cell at scholarships@woxsen.edu.in or Admin Room 203.

## Transportation & Parking

### University Bus Service
Woxsen University operates bus services from Hyderabad city on designated routes. Bus passes can be obtained from the Transport Office (Admin Block, Ground Floor, near Security Gate).
- Cost: ₹8,000 per semester (non-AC) | ₹12,000 per semester (AC)
- Application: First 2 weeks of each semester at the Transport Office

### Parking Policy
- Students with valid parking stickers may park in Zone B and Zone C.
- Parking stickers are issued by the Security Office (Block A, Ground Floor). Cost: ₹500 per year.
- Unauthorized parking will result in vehicle being towed at owner's expense.

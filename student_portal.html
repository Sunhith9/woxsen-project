Official Policy
Grievance Redressal Policy
Woxsen University — Fair, transparent & timely resolution for all students.
⚖️
Academic Grievances
Examinations, results, attendance, faculty conduct. Resolved within 7 working days by Exam Cell / HOD.
🏛️
Administrative Grievances
Admission, fees, documents, ID cards, hostel, transport. Admin / Accounts Office, 5 working days.
🏗️
Infrastructure Grievances
Classrooms, labs, Wi-Fi, sanitation, canteen, safety. Estate Officer / IT team.
🚨
Ragging Complaints
Physical, verbal, online, hostel ragging. Highest priority — resolved within 24 hours.
